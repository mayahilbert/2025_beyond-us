New Media Artspace Beyond Us Curatorial Statement

Ghost in the Machine

Dylan Shalmer

Music and interactive filter, 2025

This piece blurs the line between presence and absence, creating a dissociative experience where memories distort, glitch, and reappear like echoes from another time. By interacting with the work, viewers confront the limits of perception, stepping into a space where reality dissolves, and the remnants of what once was continue to haunt the present.

This project requires camera access.
