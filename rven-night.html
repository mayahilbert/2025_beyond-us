RVEN NIGHT

Amely Gonell

Web game, 2025

In RVEN NIGHT, step into the wings of a raven and gaze down upon the city below. This project uses motion tracking to embody the bird's perspective, allowing players to listen in on the lives of its inhabitants. A pixelated art style evokes nostalgia and comfort, contrasting with the raven's symbolic ties to loss, death, and trans- formation.
