What You Can't See

Shaima Rini

Interactive artwork, 2025

What You Can't See is an interactive artwork created by Shaima Rini that blends technology and artwork together into one piece. The artwork uses motion tracking to allow the viewer to explore the dark bedroom of a child, as images appear and disappear. Representing childhood fears of monsters lurking at the night and how the “light” was a barrier to save us from the unknown.

This project requires camera access.
